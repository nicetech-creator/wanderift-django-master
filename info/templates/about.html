About

What we offer and how it benefits you

Retail Flights

Wanderift offers what we call “Retail Flights”. Now what exactly are Retail Flights? Retail flights offer more destinations, preferred seating options, and industry wide pricing per flight. With a Retail Flight, you do not need a subscription to fly with us. Just browse, book, fly. It is as easy as that!

Personal Subscriptions

Our subscription plans are what Wanderift started with (which means we have nearly perfected the experience), and provide the biggest airline booking savings. With a Personal Subscription, you pay one of two low monthly prices, and can fly to over 24 major destinations throughout the United States such as Los Angeles, Miami, and New York to name a few. If you have any questions about our Personal Subscription plans, feel free to email us at info@wanderift.com and we will be more than happy to answer any questions you may have!

Corporate Subscriptions

Does your company find that you have to take care of business in different states to reach your quotas? That is where our Corporate Subscriptions come into play. We made this simple and easy for you to take advantage of. Whoever you want to be the account holder creates a Corporate Subscription account, and whoever they need to fly to conduct business has their account where they can pull from your allotted token pool. You can purchase 10-100 tokens at one time, and they never expire! Any questions about our Corporate Subscription plans can be answered by contacting info@wanderift.com.
