How it Works

Change up your travel plans and start saving on flights today!

We were started on our popular airline subscription plans, which many people take advantage of with the immense savings. Our airline subscriptions plans are simple… pay either $369 per month for Wanderift Lite, and get 3 one-way flights to any of over 24 destinations in the United States OR choose our Wanderift Pro plan and get 4 oneway flights for only $459 per month.

Our token system is a very simple way to book airline flights. Instead of paying a different price each flight, you pay with tokens. One token equals a single one-way flight. This makes booking your flights easier than ever before!

We use the most popular airlines on the market. You can choose from American Airlines, Delta Airlines, Southwest Airlines, as well as United Airlines. We also offer flights from Spirit, Frontier, and other airlines if you choose to do so.

Booked your flight with Wanderift, and want to learn what to do next? Simple…get your airline booking verification, and you are good to go. The verification will provide basic information such as date of flight, which airline, and what time your flight is at. This is very common on all major booking platforms.
